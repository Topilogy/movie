Computer case houses and protects other parts of computer hardware. Computer cases come in different shape and size, which the term form factors is used to described the 3 existing categories. Tower cases are usually vertical designed to sit on the floor in tower cases, the height is longer than the width. Desktop cases are usually horizontal designed to rest on the shell or sit on your desk with the monitor on top of it. In Desktop cases the width is longer than the height.
